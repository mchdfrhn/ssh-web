Voice & Tone

Brand personality, language rules & copy patterns

🏢
Professional
Credible, instansi-grade quality — not amateur
🤝
Approachable
Friendly, not intimidating — speaks UMKM language
💪
Confident
Direct, assertive — "tanpa drama"
🎯
Practical
Results-focused — "dari ide sampai deploy"
🔍
Transparent
Honest pricing, clear timelines — "harga transparan"

Language Rules

Primary Language Indonesian (Bahasa Indonesia) — English for brand guide docs
Tone Casual-professional. Not stiff corporate, not overly slang.
Technical Terms OK in context (React, API, deploy) — audience understands
English Terms Use for tech terms naturally. Don't force-translate "landing page"
Perspective First-person plural ("kami") for company voice

✓ Words to Use

sistem website digital cepat tepat transparan UMKM klinik bisnis lokal deploy maintenance garansi estimasi gratis dari ide sampai deploy

✕ Words to Avoid

solusi revolusioner disruptif inovatif enterprise-grade implementasi berkelanjutan commitment-free trial end-to-end synergistic best-in-class leverage paradigm shift
